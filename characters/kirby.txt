Kirby
----------
* Attributes
    * Max air speed increased massively
    * Air acceleration increased massively
    * Initial dash and run speeds increased
    * Walk speed increased
    * Crouch hurtbox made consistent (no longer bounces)
    * Kirby having powers knocked out of him is no longer random. If Kirby takes 60% since gaining a power, he will drop it
* Side special
    * Sweetspot made slightly easier to hit
    * Sourspot knockback increased
    * Slight startup reduction. Great for punishing reads
* Neutral special
    * Grab hitbox comes out a few frames sooner (17 -> 13)
    * Swallowing endlag reduced
    * Swallowing puts opponents in hitstun/tumble
    * All changes to character's stolen special moves apply to Kirby's moves as well
* Down special
    * Startup animation shortened
    * Falling speed increased
    * Can be canceled sooner
    * Cancel animation length shortened massively
* Forward air
    * Time between each hit decreased by a few frames
* Down air
    * Slight startup reduction
* Neutral air
    * Startup decreased massively (10 -> 4)
    * Dur decreased, endlag decreased
    * Perpetually functions as a weak hit nair
* Dash attack
    * Movement speed increased
    * BKB increased on all hits
    * Duration decreased
* Up throw
    * Will no longer leave stage
    * No longer takes away Kirby's jumps, allowing him to get aerial followups
* Forward throw, back throw
    * Will no longer leave stage
    * Can no longer be mashed out of
