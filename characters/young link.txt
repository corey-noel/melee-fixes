Young Link

    * BKB increased
    * Cool-down reduced
    * Pointblank arrow sourspot removed
* Side special
    * Returning boomerang KB increased
    * Boomerang catch made entirely interruptible
* Down air
    * Landing lag decreased (50 -> 32)
* Up smash
    * Hitboxes moved further down on YL to hit grounded opponents
    * SKB tweaked to better combo
* Forward smash
    * Second hit can be used much sooner
* Grabs
    * Standing grab
        * End lag reduced by 15 frames (85 -> 70)
    * Dash grab
        * End lag reduced by 15 frames (95 -> 80)
* Forward throw
    * BKB and KBG increased
* Back throw
    * KBG increased
