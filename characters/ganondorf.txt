Ganondorf
---------------
* Attributes
    * Initial dash speed increased
    * Max air speed and air drift increased slightly
    * Jump squat reduced to 5
    * Natural landing lag reduced to 4
    * Gains slightly more height from double jump
    * Spotdodge made 17/27
* Up special
    * Increased edge grab box size, allowing sweetspotting under more moves
* Side special
    * Grounded side special angle changed to 90
    * Aerial launch angle changed to 340 degrees (slightly below straight forward)
    * BKB and KBG increased on both aerial and grounded versions increased
* Neutral air
    * Hitbox duration increased on both hits
    * First hit KBG decreased and made more vertical
    * Landing lag decreased
* Down air
    * Landing lag decreased
* Up tilt
    * Made into a normal axe kick, like Samus' or Falcon's, but a bit slower and a bit more disjointed
    * Legs are intangible from a few frames before the hitbox through the duration of the hitbox
    * Meteors grounded opponents, sends aerial ones up and away
    * Massive shield DMG
    * 19%
    * Very high BKB
